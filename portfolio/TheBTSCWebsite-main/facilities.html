Ballroom

The Bridgehampton Tennis & Surf Club can accommodate up to 240 people for a formal sit down dinner and dancing or 400 for a cocktail party. The ballroom connects to wrap around ocean and pool decks so that your guests will enjoy panoramic views of the ocean and bay. Our bar overlooks the ocean allowing your guests to enjoy breathtaking views while drinking delicious cocktails. Given the unique location, you can watch the summer sunset with 180 degree views.
